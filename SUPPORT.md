# EcoDrive Support

For general support, email **admin@jarsoft.co.nz** or use the public issue forms in this repository.

Use:

- **Bug report** for reproducible product defects;
- **Feature request** for proposed improvements; and
- **Security email** for vulnerabilities or suspected security defects.

Before opening a public issue, remove personal or sensitive information. Do not publish access tokens, purchase tokens, email addresses, contact details, registration plates, VINs, exact home addresses, or unredacted trip logs and screenshots containing precise routes.

EcoDrive is a driving-assistance tool. For urgent safety, emergency, collision, vehicle, legal, billing-account, or roadside matters, use the appropriate emergency service, vehicle professional, Google Play support channel, or other qualified provider rather than GitHub Issues.
