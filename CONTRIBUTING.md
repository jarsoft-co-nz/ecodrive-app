# Contributing to EcoDrive public resources

This public repository contains support, policy, issue-tracking, and documentation material for EcoDrive. The application source is maintained privately by Jarsoft Limited.

## Issues

Use the structured issue forms and provide the minimum information needed to understand the problem. Remove personal information before submitting logs, screenshots, routes, or diagnostic material.

Public issues must not contain credentials, purchase tokens, private contact data, registration plates, VINs, exact home addresses, or unredacted trip histories.

## Pull requests

Pull requests may propose corrections or improvements to public documentation. Keep changes focused, explain why they are needed, and do not add application source, confidential material, third-party copyrighted content, or claims about EcoDrive behaviour that are not supported by the current product.

Legal policies remain subject to review and approval by Jarsoft Limited. A contribution does not create a professional, employment, partnership, or agency relationship with Jarsoft.

## Conduct

Be respectful, factual, and safety-conscious. Do not use the repository to harass others, disclose private information, promote unsafe driving, or solicit access to private systems or data.
