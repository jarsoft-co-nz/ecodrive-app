## Support topics

### Permissions

EcoDrive may request precise location, Bluetooth, notifications, and optional contacts access. Contacts search is off by default. Permissions can be declined, but the related feature may not work.

### Trip data

Trip, GPS, OBD-II, vehicle, and preference data is primarily stored on the device. Complete trip logs are not automatically uploaded to Jarsoft. Users should redact private locations and identifiers before sharing exports or screenshots.

### Navigation and routing

Route requests send origin, destination, and stop coordinates to Jarsoft routing services. Address lookup may use the Android geocoding provider or OpenStreetMap Nominatim.

### Subscriptions

Subscriptions are purchased and managed through Google Play. Jarsoft processes pseudonymous entitlement information but does not receive payment-card details.

### Reporting a problem

Use the repository's structured issue forms for reproducible bugs and feature requests. Send vulnerabilities and non-public support matters to admin@jarsoft.co.nz.
